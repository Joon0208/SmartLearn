Frequently Asked Questions

Need help? We've got you covered!


Payment & Orders

Ordering and Payment

All ordering is processed online at www.yammy.com

Customers are billed directly on the website. Order cut off time is on every friday, 12 noon.

Opening Hours

Our Opening hours are Monday to Friday 9:00AM to 5:00PM. Any emails/inquiries outside these hours will receive response within the next business day.

We are closed on public holidays. No deliveries will be made during these times.

Do you have a refund policy?

Refunds are subjected to approval by the management. We reserve the right to reject any refund with appropriate reasons.

If you are requesting to cancel your order that is currently being processed, you must contact us within 3 working days. Else we will not accept any changes or cancellations after the 3 working days period.

Are there any GST charges?

Yes. All prices are subject to prevailing GST.

Shipment

Will there be a delivery fee?

For our plan, we charge a delivery fee of $4 per delivery trip.

Eco-friendly boxes

1. Our meal boxes are made from sugar cane and is 100% bio-degradable.

2. Our bags are made from recycled paper, and we encouraged our customers to re-use them whenever possible.

Delivery

We deliver daily from Monday to Friday (except public holiday and weekend). Our minimum order is 5 days consecutively Monday to Friday, and we reserved the rights to apply extra delivery charges on special requests.

Please note that we DO NOT deliver to the following areas:

  • All Airports and military camps
  • Prisons
  • Offshore islands including Sentosa
  • All special pass-protected areas and Tuas
  • Sentosa Marina
